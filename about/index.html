About

Welcome to One Stop Pinball Shop

Passion for Pinball, Crafted with Precision

As a passionate pinball enthusiast and maker, I’ve always believed that every pinball machine should reflect its owner’s personality. That’s why I started One Stop Pinball Shop - to bring custom, high-quality 3D printed mods and accessories to fellow pinball lovers across Europe.

Every product you see here is designed and crafted by me in my workshop in Assen, The Netherlands. Using professional-grade 3D printing technology, I create unique toppers, playfield mods, and personalized accessories that transform standard machines into truly one-of-a-kind creations.

What sets us apart? It’s simple: personal attention, quality craftsmanship, and affordable customization. No mass production, no compromises - just genuine passion for pinball translated into products you’ll love.
